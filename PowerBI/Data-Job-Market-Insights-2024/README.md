# Data Job Market Insights (2024)

## Overview
This project analyzes the 2024 data job market with a focus on Data Analyst roles. 
The dashboard is designed to help users understand hiring trends, salary distribution, and role-specific employment characteristics using interactive visual analytics.

## Key Insights
- Data Analyst job postings declined after August and recovered toward December 2024  
- Median yearly salary for Data Analyst roles is approximately $90K, with hourly rates around $32.50  
- Higher yearly salaries generally align with higher hourly compensation  
- Full-time positions represent the majority of data job opportunities  
- Job postings are concentrated in major technology hubs and regions  
- Remote work availability and degree requirements vary across job listings  

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive job role selection using slicers  
- KPI cards for job volume and salary overview  
- Trend analysis of hiring activity across months  
- Salary comparison across selected roles  
- Geographic distribution of job opportunities  
- Drill-through navigation for detailed role-level exploration  

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Data Visualization and Dashboard Design)
